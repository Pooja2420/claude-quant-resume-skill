Master keyword list for Tier-1 ATS systems (Amazon, Citadel, Jane Street, Meta, Microsoft,
Google, Netflix, IMC, Nasdaq, Apple, Walmart, CVS). Inject relevant terms naturally into
bullets, skills, and coursework sections.

## ATS Formatting Notes

- Spell out acronyms at least once: "High-Frequency Trading (HFT)"
- Use both spelled-out and abbreviated forms: "machine learning (ML)"
- Match the job description language exactly — if JD says "quantitative research",
  do not write "quant research"
- Section headers ATS can parse: EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, SKILLS, PROJECTS,
  PUBLICATIONS, AWARDS — do NOT use creative headers like "My Story"
